Install the practical upgrades that make your home work better.

Help with common home fixtures, mounting tasks, and small installation projects that need careful measuring and clean placement.

Installations with a clean, clear scope

Fixture installation can include curtain rods, blinds, towel bars, cabinet pulls, shelves, hooks, house numbers, simple bathroom accessories, and similar household items. These projects are often small, but good placement, anchors, level alignment, and wall type matter.

For electrical fixtures, plumbing fixtures, heavy mounting, or anything tied to code requirements, the business details should clearly state whether the job is accepted, referred out, or requires a licensed professional.

Helpful details to send

  • Product link or instructions
  • Wall type if known
  • Item weight and dimensions
  • Preferred placement photos
