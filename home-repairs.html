What this service covers

Small repairs are ideal for homeowners, renters, landlords, and property managers who need practical help with everyday issues. This can include loose hardware, sticking doors, cabinet adjustments, damaged trim, basic caulking, minor wall damage, squeaky hinges, and similar non-structural repairs.

The goal is to keep the scope clear, finish the work neatly, and avoid unnecessary complexity. If a repair looks like it requires a licensed trade, permit, or deeper inspection, the site should clearly direct visitors to the right specialist instead of overpromising.

Helpful details to send

  • Photos of the repair
  • Approximate location in the home
  • How long the issue has been happening
  • Any parts already purchased
