Drywall & paint touch-ups

Clean up small wall damage without turning it into a major project.

Focused drywall repair and paint touch-up help for small holes, scuffs, dents, and move-out wall damage.

Wall repairs that stay practical

Drywall and paint touch-ups are best for smaller damage that needs a clean, presentable finish. This may include nail holes, small dents, minor cracks, scuffed corners, anchor damage, and patched areas after shelves or fixtures are removed.

Paint matching can vary based on wall age, sheen, lighting, and existing paint condition. For best results, visitors should provide the original paint color or a paint sample when available.

Before the visit

  • Share close-up and full-wall photos
  • Confirm whether paint is available
  • Note wall texture if known
  • Clear furniture from the work area
