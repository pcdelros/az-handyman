Careful assembly for everyday furniture

Furniture assembly is a good fit for desks, beds, shelves, bookcases, patio sets, garage storage, entryway benches, TV stands, and similar boxed products. This service is designed for practical setup, readable instructions, and keeping parts organized during assembly.

Visitors should keep the product in the assembly area when possible and share a product link or model number before the appointment. Very large, wall-mounted, custom, or commercial furniture may need extra review before scheduling.

Before assembly

  • Keep instructions and hardware together
  • Clear enough floor space
  • Share the product link
  • Confirm if wall anchoring is needed
